Dogs info


The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it was domesticated from an extinct population of Pleistocene wolves over 14,000 years ago. The dog was the first species to be domesticated by humans.


Dogs


Dogs are the most variable mammal on Earth, with artificial selection producing upward of 360 globally recognized breeds.

Dog stats

  • Lifespan: 10 – 13 years
  • Gestation period: 58 – 68 days
  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Domain: Eukaryota
  • Family: Canidae
